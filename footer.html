About

COVID-19 real-time information, reporting cases, testing, and exposure sites in Australia. The system comprises four main components:

  • Map of Exposure Sites
  • Automated Alerts System
  • Data Visualisation Dashboards
  • Data Engine
This system provides integrated data from multiple sources in one platform which optimises information sharing with public health responders, primary health care practitioners and the general public.

CRISPER is a collaborative project between

  • ANU Research School of Population Health;
  • ANU Software Innovation Institute;
  • ANU GRAPHC;
  • UQ School of Public Health;
Funding
